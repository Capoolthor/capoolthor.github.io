I was born on June 10, 2008, at Angeles Hospital in Ciudad Juarez, Chihuahua, Mexico.

My beginning in soccer.
Initially, I had no interest in soccer as such, until some friends made it more appealing to me. That's how I got to know my team, "VALLE DEL SOL." At the age of 8, I joined this team where I experienced the passion for soccer. We were considered the best or one of the best; we won a lot of awards and trophies, until I got injured. At that moment, I went through a tough time, fell into a kind of depression, and gained weight.
