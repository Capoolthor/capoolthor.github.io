WHO I AM? ...
A profound and challenging question.
I am a reserved individual but can easily interact with anyone if the person I am interacting with wants to get to know me. Upon getting to know me, I tend to be an interesting person who can discuss various topics and intertwine them with more subjects.
I enjoy experimenting with various things, such as food, experiences in the sense of enjoying an activity like a game or more adrenaline-fueled pursuits, among other things.
I am someone you can engage with and have a good time learning and enjoying, whether it's a fun moment or times when help is needed. In those moments, I can be a friend who listens to you in your times of need and offers perspectives on what is right or wrong, always supporting you in your decisions.
I am sociable but also reserved, which complicates my interpersonal relationships to some extent.
However, this also supports my ability to understand the decisions of those close to me in the context of sincere friendship.
I have the quality of excelling in most things, thanks to my adaptability in physical, mental, and emotional activities.
I love learning and discovering a wide range of things. I also enjoy cooking, drawing, photography, and more. I consider myself an honest and sincere person, although it is challenging for me to open up if it's not with the right person. I also have ambition, by which I mean I seek to improve as a person, student, and friend.
Personally, I aim to be and give my best in any situation, to discover myself as an individual, achieve goals, and evolve in every sense for the future.
